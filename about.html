About

This page highlights my academic interests and future goals.

Business and Technology

I am studying Business and Information Technology because I care about how technology creates real value for people and organizations. I want to understand both the technical side of building systems and the business side of making them useful, scalable, and sustainable.

My long term goal is to use technology to reduce barriers to opportunity. That includes making tools more accessible and helping people who are often left behind by rapid changes in technology.

Background

I am focused on growth through consistency. I take my commitments seriously and I am building discipline through school, work, and projects that require long term effort. I want my work to reflect precision and accountability.

I value leadership and service, and I am interested in opportunities that let me combine technical skills with community impact. I want to become the type of person people can rely on when there is real work to be done.

Interests

  • Web development and user focused design
  • Programming and problem solving
  • Business strategy and how companies create value
  • Accessibility and inclusive technology
  • Leadership and team collaboration
  • Building projects that solve practical problems
  • Learning systems that scale and remain reliable

Goals

I want to build strong fundamentals in web development by writing valid HTML that is structured and easy to read. I also want to improve how I communicate ideas so that my work is clear to both technical and non technical audiences.

Over time, I want to apply these skills to projects that improve access to technology and education. I care about measurable impact, not just building things that look good.
